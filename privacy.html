FaithClock Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

FaithClock is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles your information.

Information We Collect

FaithClock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Local Data Only

All app data stays on your device:

  • Settings Preferences: Your chosen fonts, text sizes, and display options are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage.
  • Photo Library Access: FaithClock requests access to your Photo Library solely to display photos as backgrounds within the app. Your photos are never uploaded, shared, or transmitted anywhere.

No Analytics or Tracking

FaithClock does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

No Account Required

FaithClock does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

Children's Privacy

FaithClock does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will post the new version here with an updated date.
